Who We Are?

We are a team of bioinformatics students from the Hanze University of Applied Sciences who are passionate about making bioinformatics tools more accessible to students, scientists, and researchers across various disciplines. Our mission is to empower users with the ability to easily retrieve and visualize metabolic and genetic data through KEGG tools.

Our Vision

Our goal is to bridge the gap between complex biological data and researchers by providing an intuitive platform that integrates KEGG’s powerful resources. We strive to create a space where biological data can be effortlessly visualized, analyzed, and interpreted, thus helping researchers and students better understand biological processes.

What We Offer

Map My KEGG provides a web-based interface where users can:

• Access detailed genetic and metabolic data from KEGG

• Visualize and analyze biological pathways and processes

• Integrate experimental data with KEGG's known biological networks

The Tools We Use

KEGG Pull

KEGG Pull is a Python-based tool designed to retrieve biological data from KEGG’s extensive database. This tool allows users to access genetic sequences, metabolic pathways, and orthologs, offering flexibility and efficiency through its Application Programming Interface (API) and command-line interface (CLI).

KEGG Mapper

KEGG Mapper is a powerful suite of tools used for visualizing biological pathways, integrating experimental data, and mapping genetic information. With features like Reconstruct, Search, Color, and Join, KEGG Mapper helps users explore and analyze complex biological networks, enabling a deeper understanding of cellular processes.
